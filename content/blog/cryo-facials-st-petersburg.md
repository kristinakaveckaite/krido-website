A cryo facial uses controlled cold to help tighten and brighten the skin, all in a quick, relaxing session with no downtime. Most clients describe it as a refreshing cooling sensation rather than anything uncomfortable.

## What to expect

A typical session takes about 30 minutes. There's no recovery time afterward — many people notice a fresh glow right away, with results that build over a series of visits.

## Is it right for you?

Cryo facials are completely non-invasive, which makes them a popular choice for anyone curious about firmer, more radiant skin without surgery. If you have questions about whether it suits your skin, our team is happy to talk it through at your consultation.
